Here is our list for TOP 10 games of all time:

RECOMMENDATIONS

The Uncharted Series:

The Uncharted series is famous for its iconic treasure hunting gameplay. It is a perfect combination of parkour, shooting, problem solving, hidden obbjects and intellectual puzzles. Each game has its unique mystery, plot-twists, weapons and locations. We guarentee that you won't have to reconsider your choice with this masterpiece. (9.3/10)

The Assassin's Creed:

Assassin's Creed is the most amazing open world game series. This saga focuses on exploring the past of assassins by using futuristic animus technology. It shows rise and fall of great cities and people over time. Each aspect of clothes, weapons, items, locations, mounts etc. is created to resemble the real world with utmost accuracy and precision. (9.8/10)

God Of War Saga:

As the name suggests, this game is based on the God of war Kratos who travelled through greek and norse mythology for the sake of our gaming experience. This series is famous for its unique fighting style and violence for the sake of vengence. The mythologies have been redefined to sanctify the players, such is the power of this atrocious game. (8.7/10)

Halo Collecton:

Halo has been one of the most vibrant games of all time. The visual effects, graphics and soundtracks are second to no one. This series has been an eye candy for everyone who ever laid hands on it. The iconic story of rescuing the entire cosmos from total destruction and alien harmony is like fullfilled fantasy. Don't believe us? Then try it yourself. (9.5/10)

Prince Of Persia:

This majestic series has been through thick and thin. This story takes you across time to the flourishing past of persia filled with mysteries and treasures. You will be spending more time finding the right paths in this game than your real life. Immerse youself in an experience that beats time on each & every corner, for the weapons are to be killed for. (8.9/10)

The Last Of Us Duo:

Set in the post apocalyptic world, this game is an emotional rollercoaster. From evading zombies, fighting for survival, crafting to puzzle solving, this duo has it all. The iconic bagpack feature for collecting items and weapon handelling put you in a trance of adventure. So grab your controller, dim the lights and turn on the sound cause shit just got real. (9.7/10)
